Flexible Part Printing

01

TPU Flexible Parts

Thermoplastic polyurethane (TPU) printed via FDM and SLS for production-grade flexible parts. Shore 85A standard, 95A available. Tear resistance, abrasion resistance, and chemical compatibility for industrial seal and gasket applications.

02

Silicone Printing

Two-part platinum-cure silicone printed via direct ink writing (DIW). Biocompatible, autoclavable, and chemically inert. Shore 10A to 60A hardness. Wearable sensors, medical device seals, and food-contact components.

03

Overmold Simulation

PolyJet rigid-rubber overmold simulation in a single print. Evaluate grip ergonomics, IP seal design, and vibration isolation performance before committing to injection mold tooling.
